## GOAL

When Arabic/Bangla font size (or leading, tracking, hScale) changes, or text
overflows a row, linked layers reflow (word-wrap cascade) per:
- Linking switch (arabic / bangla) ON or OFF
- Editor scope: general | page | surah | global

Link OFF → overflow clips on current row + toast (no spill to next row).
